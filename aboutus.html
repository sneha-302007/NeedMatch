About NeedMatch

NeedMatch is a transparent and trusted donation platform that connects donors with verified NGOs, orphanages, and old age homes across India. Our goal is to simplify the donation process while ensuring every contribution reaches the people who need it most.

Whether you’re donating food, clothes, or daily essentials, NeedMatch ensures your donation is delivered securely and efficiently. We charge 0% platform fee, so your generosity makes the biggest impact possible.

We believe every act of kindness matters, and we're here to help you make it count.

Our Mission

NeedMatch bridges the gap between donors and NGOs running orphanages and old age homes. We make sure donations are delivered transparently, efficiently, and meaningfully to bring real impact where it’s needed most.

How NeedMatch Works

1. NGO Registration

NGOs create profiles by entering their name, location, mission, multimedia, and needs.

2. Find & Filter

Donors search NGOs using filters like type (old age home/orphanage), what to donate, and location—no login needed.

3. Connect & Donate

Donors fill a form; NGOs receive it, confirm the donation, and send a receipt with a unique donation ID.

4. Secure Follow-up

After donation, donors get thank you messages, photos, and future updates on urgent needs.

Making Every Act of Kindness Count

Every donation tells a story. NeedMatch makes sure your kindness reaches those who need it most, safely and transparently.
