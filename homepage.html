How It Works?

For Donors For NGOs

CHOOSE A NGO

Browse different NGOs/ TRUSTs and select one to DONATE.

➔

CHECK INFORMATION

Check the informations/history/needs of NGO.

➔

FILL UP THE FORM

Checkout and fill the information asked in form and submit it.

➔

DELIVERY OF PRODUCTS

Get a secret code from NGO through mail and handover the products to pickup person.

➔

THANKYOU REPORT

SIGNUP/ LOGIN

➔

CREATE PROFILE

KNOW DONORS

➔

RECEIVE PRODUCTS

SEND REPORT
