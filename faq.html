Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is there any platform fee?

No. NeedMatch does not charge any platform fee. 100% of your donation goes to the NGO.

2. How are NGOs verified?

All listed NGOs undergo a strict verification process including document checks and activity validation.

3. Do I need to create an account to donate?

No registration is required to donate. You can simply choose a cause and contribute.

4. Can I track my donation?

Yes. NGOs send a detailed Thank You report after receiving your donation.
